About the Azerbaijan Volleyball Federation

Azerbaijan Volleyball Fan Club (AVFC)

is a unique public platform that brings together volleyball enthusiasts. Founded in 2025, this fan club aims to popularize volleyball in Azerbaijan and unite volleyball fans into one community.

Our Goals

  • To promote volleyball throughout Azerbaijan
  • To strengthen fan loyalty to the National Volleyball Team and clubs
  • To organize engaging and exciting events for fans
  • To expand volleyball's reach through digital platforms

Fan Club Offers

After clicking the JOIN button and becoming a member, you can benefit from the following free offers:

  • European trips to attend National Volleyball Team matches
  • Foreign language courses and speaking practice
  • Professional volleyball training sessions and games

Main Objectives

To create an excellent platform for volleyball lovers in Azerbaijan, increase fan engagement, and help youth avoid harmful activities by involving them in healthy and purposeful sports.
