Azerrail Baku – One of the Leading Volleyball Clubs in Azerbaijan

Azerrail Baku is one of the most recognized names in Azerbaijani volleyball, standing out with the achievements of both its women's and men's teams on local and international stages.

The club's women's team was established in 2001. Since its inception, the Azerrail women's team has risen to a leading position in Azerbaijani volleyball and has proudly represented the country on the European stage. The team has won prestigious international tournaments such as the CEV Cup (2002) and the CEV Challenge Cup (2011), and has claimed the championship title multiple times in the Azerbaijan Super League.

In 2019, the club's men's team was founded. Formed in a short period, this team developed rapidly and became the champion of Azerbaijan in the 2023–2024 season, making its mark at the top of the nation's volleyball scene.

Murov Az Terminal – Azerbaijan’s Emerging Volleyball Club

Murov Az Terminal is one of the new and rising names in Azerbaijani volleyball, drawing attention with the achievements of both its women's and men's teams.

The club's women's team was established in 2019. In a short period, the Murov Az Terminal women's team has become one of the strong competitors in Azerbaijani volleyball. The team has performed successfully both locally and internationally and has earned honors in the Azerbaijan Super League.

The club's men's team also began competing in 2019. The Murov Az Terminal men's team quickly proved itself and became the champion of Azerbaijan in the 2023–2024 season, achieving remarkable results.
